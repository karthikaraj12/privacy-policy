Privacy Policy for Transit Arrival Alert

Last Updated: March 7, 2026

Thank you for using Transit Arrival Alert. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Location Information

Transit Arrival Alert uses your device’s location (GPS) to provide the core functionality of the app.

The app collects location data in order to:

  • Track your travel progress
  • Determine when you are approaching your selected destination stop
  • Trigger an alert before you reach your stop

Location tracking may continue in the background while the app is running so the alert feature can function properly.

Personal Information

Transit Arrival Alert does not collect or store personal information such as:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Contacts

Device Information

Basic device information such as operating system version or device type may be used by the system for app performance and compatibility.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information collected is used only to:

  • Detect when you are approaching your selected stop
  • Trigger arrival alerts
  • Ensure the app functions correctly

Your data is not used for advertising, profiling, or tracking across other apps or websites.

3. Data Storage

Transit Arrival Alert processes location data locally on your device.

The app does not store location data on external servers and does not maintain travel history.

4. Data Sharing

Transit Arrival Alert does not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties.

Location data is used only for the core functionality of the application.

5. Permissions Used

To function correctly, the app may request the following permissions:

  • Location Permission (Foreground & Background) – to monitor your travel progress and trigger alerts.
  • Notification Permission – to send loud alerts when you approach your destination.

These permissions are required for the main functionality of the app.

6. Children's Privacy

Transit Arrival Alert is not directed toward children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
